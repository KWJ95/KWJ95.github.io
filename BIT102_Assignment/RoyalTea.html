Royal Tea

About Royaltea Malaysia

Royaltea 皇茶 has more than 130 outlets in China, and the first to bring authentic cheese tea to Malaysian shores. Royaltea serves a wide array of high quality tea from Sun Moon Lake (日月潭) while also not forgetting coffee lovers.

General Information

Our concepts

With the concept of "natural, healthy and delicious", we seek to create a new style of leisure drinks and food. Our specialty is making our drinks with modern technology an infusing with traditional charm. By doing this and adding different elements, we constantly come up with changing flavours.We incorporate our concept in our shop's decoration as well, giving our patrons a sense of return to nature, while drinking the best tea on offer, thus bringing the best relaxation.

Our Mission

Royaltea hopes to be able to combine leisure and culture by bringing high quality tea drinks and snacks to places near you.

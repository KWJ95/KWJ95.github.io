KOI

About KOI

Inheriting the spirit of Taiwan 50 Lan's founder and its unbeatened position as Taiwan's market leader, KOI's brand is focused on bringing out the following: selection of raw material, tea-brewing process, precision and consistency, and friendly service.

General Information

Why KOI

Their tea not only tastes extraordinary on your first sip, it would leave you craving for more even after you have finished. To achieve this, thier baristas have to undergo stringent training. Only thorugh this pursuit of perfection that you may continue to enjoy the soothing aftertaste in every sip.

KOI's Promise

KOI strvies to provide the most friendly services by treating you as part of their family. They hope to provide an oasis of warmth and serenity as you enjoy your customised beverage while you break away from busy life.

Menu

Product Overview

KOI has fully grasped the art and expertise of cooking tapioca pearls, and theirs are the crown jewel in every KOI product. No matter the time of the day, their pearls are kept chewy and soft, bringing you a mouthful of gold when you take a sip.

Golden Bubble
Milk Tea

essentially a national treasure from Taiwan, their rich and creamy milk tea is further elevated to next level when combined with their golden bubbles.

Looks exactly like the Golden Bubble Milk Tea but it is not. This is a delight unique to KOI Thé – featuring fragrant, chewy taro balls in their classic milk tea. A generous dollop of these purple balls are plopped into the cup, and one serving is enough to fill you up. Don’t worry about all the starch you’ll be consuming – the dainty taro balls are made with real yams with yam bits for you chew on.

Recommended at a 70% sugar level, the Matcha Latte is a milkier option for y’all Matchaholics. Creamy milk fills the bottom of the cup and is carefully layered on top with slightly bitter matcha. When mixed together, the milk neutralises the bitterness of the matcha, producing an overall decadent flavour that does not get old.

Matcha Latte

Their matcha powder contains no preservatives. It neutralizes with the smooth fresh milk to achieve the signature luscious green tea latte texture.

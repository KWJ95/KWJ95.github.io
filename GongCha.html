Gong cha

About Gong cha

Gong Cha 貢茶 is an international bubble tea chain that originated from Kaohsiung, Taiwan in 2006, and since 2009, has swept Malaysia’s neighboring countries – Singapore, Hong Kong, China, Macau and the Philippines – by storm. To date, Gong Cha is the fastest growing brand and has further expanded internationally to South Korea, Japan, USA, Canada and other countries, with more than 1,500 stores worldwide.

General Information

Why Gong cha

Cooperating with professional factories, they harvest only tea leaves of the best quality with a taste unique to Gong Cha. Every batch of tea distributed is certified by SGS International which is the world's leading inspection, verification, testing and certification company. Moreover, their commitment to freshness is uncompromising as they brew their teas every four hours for optimal flavour and freshness.

Gong cha's Promise

They adopt a high standard of water purification facilities, where the water they use to brew tea, produce ice cubes and prepare drinks are totally bactericidal. Consumers may rest assured that all of their products are hygienic and safe. They also value your wants and needs, so they tailor every cup to your individual taste and sweetness preference.
